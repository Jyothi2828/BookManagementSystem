Help - Book Page

Welcome to the Help section for the Book Page. Here, you will find answers to common questions and guidance on how to use the features available on the Book page.

Frequently Asked Questions

  • How do I search for a book?

    To search for a book, use the search bar at the top of the page. Enter the book title, author, or keyword and press the search icon to get results.

  • How do I add a book to my collection?

    Click the "Add Book" button and fill in the required details about the book. Once completed, click the "Save" button to add the book.

  • How do I edit or update book information?

    To edit book details, click the pencil icon next to the book entry. Make the necessary changes and save the updates.

  • How do I delete a book from my collection?

    To delete a book, click the trash bin icon next to the book entry. Confirm the deletion when prompted.
